Frist, choose your interest fields. Then click "Load the dataset" button, choose a
corresponding .csv file, you will get a table showing your interest fields.

Second, enter the starting time, ending time, time step (in minute), stationID (0~80),
total time(in minute), Inflow/Outflow(1/0).Then click "Plot the traffic flow" button, 
you will get a chart showing the inflow/outflow.

Third, enter the entering stationID, exiting stationID. Then click "Plot the route" 
button, choose a corresponding .csv file, you will get a series of routes.
